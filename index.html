About me

Pharmacist, regulatory affairs professional and responsible AI learner.

I bring more than two decades of medicines regulation experience to this portfolio, with a focus on practical, transparent and human-led AI support for regulatory science and research productivity.

Professional focus

My work has included screening Finished Pharmaceutical Product dossiers for completeness, monitoring assessment pipelines, maintaining regulatory records, supporting WHO Public Assessment Report updates, and strengthening quality management through SOPs, work instructions and guidance documents.
